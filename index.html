About Say less.

Offering

I bring strategic, empowering editorial support to personal statements, application materials, professional narratives and more. I love working with:

  • Undergraduate, graduate, professional, and post-bacc program applicants
  • Fellowship and scholarship applicants
  • Nonprofits and community organizations seeking funding
  • Professionals preparing statements of purpose, bios, or career narratives

By tailoring support around your voice and goals, I can help you:

  • Identify your core message
  • Strengthen structure and narrative flow
  • Cut filler and repetition
  • Preserve voice while elevating tone
  • Make your writing more focused and compelling

Here's how

I only accept one project at a time to give each story the attention it deserves. Here’s what to expect:

  1. You fill out the intake form — including a draft/outline/notes (it doesn’t need to be perfect!)
  2. If there’s alignment and I have capacity, I’ll follow up to arrange a phone or video call
  3. We chat to clarify your needs and direction
  4. We revise together over Google Docs

Estimated time from you: 2-3 hours

I bring the writing expertise, you bring the experiences that matter!

About me.

I hold a B.A. in English from UC Santa Barbara and work as a Program Manager at LinkedIn Learning.

For more than a decade, I’ve been the go-to ideator and editor for family, friends, and colleagues — helping them sharpen their story to unlock big, high-stakes opportunities. That’s why Say less isn’t a business; it’s a personal initiative with no cost and no catch.

If you’re building something that matters, I’d be honored to help you write your way toward it!
